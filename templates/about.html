About LegalEase Nepal

Our mission is to bridge the justice gap in Nepal's informal sector through accessible technology

Who We Are

LegalEase Nepal is an innovative legal tech initiative focused on making legal knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of income or education level.

Our team combines legal expertise with cutting-edge AI technology to create solutions that empower Nepal's small business owners and independent workers.

The Problem We Solve

In Nepal, over 60% of the workforce operates in the informal sector with little to no access to affordable legal services.

This leaves small vendors, freelancers, and micro-entrepreneurs vulnerable to exploitation, contract disputes, and legal challenges they don't understand.

Our Innovative Solution

How LegalEase Nepal is transforming access to justice through technology
